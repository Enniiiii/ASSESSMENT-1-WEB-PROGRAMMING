This is the recipe for Mokou's Funky pizza

This recipe will showcase how to make a classic cheese pizza NYC style.

Ingredients needed to make the pizza:

  • For the dough itself
    • 2 1/4 cups of warm water
    • 1 tbsp of sugar
    • 1 tbsp of active dry yeast
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp of kosher salt
    • 5 cups of bread flour
    • Extra oil for greasing the dough ball
    • Cornmeal or semolina flour for dusting
  • For the Sauce
    • 1 28 oz of can crushed tomatos
    • 2-4 tbsp of olive oil
    • 1 tsp of sugar
    • 2 tsp of dried oregano
  • For the cheese itself
    • 24-32 oz of whole mil, low moisture mozarella, freshly grated
    • grated parmesan

Method

  1. Combine water, sugar and yeast in a large bowl and leave it for a few minutes.
  2. Once it's foamy, proceed with adding olive oil, salt and 5 cups of bread flour.
  3. Mix until it is fully combined, then start kneading.
  4. Knead until you can stretch the dough without it tearing. Add flour to keep the dough not so sticky.
  5. Divide the dough into four equal pieces and store in the fridge and let them rise from 2hrs to 7 days.
  6. You are going to need a pizza stone/steel and put that into the oven. Preheat the oven to the highest possible temperature.
  7. Mix all of the sauce ingredients in a separate bowl.
  8. Dust the pizza peel with cornmeal or semolina flour.
  9. Place the pizza dough onto the peel and stretch it as wide as possible, does not have to be a perfect circle.
  10. Coat the top of the dough with sauce.
  11. Dust the top with parmesan cheese.
  12. Sprinkle the top with mozarella cheese.
  13. Transfer the pizza into the oven and bake until the crust or cheese has been browned.

Now go and enjoy your very own homemade NYC style pizza!
